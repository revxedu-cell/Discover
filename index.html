Framework

What Makes This Different

Not a project. A structure for learning.

1
Real challenge Learning is anchored in an actual problem that matters beyond school.
2

3
Real action Students design and implement solutions that create visible impact.
Improve air quality for families with asthma
Redesign public spaces for safety and use
Reduce food waste in the community

Design the Work

Frame a question that leads to action

How might we address [a real challenge] so that [a specific community] can [experience improvement]?

Make it real Ground it in a real place, partner, or need.
Keep it open Students should need to investigate before responding.
Point to action The question should lead to building, testing, or improving something.
Name who it serves Keep the people affected visible throughout the work.
